Research & Interest

Gene expression regulation is the most essential regulation for all living organisms. We use budding yeast as the model organism to study the cis regulatory logic of gene expression regulation, i.e. how transcription factors, nucleosome and polymerases, etc interact with each other and with DNA to coordinate gene expression. More specifically, we are currently focusing on modeling where and to what extend different proteins will bind at the genome, using data from both in vivo and in vitro experiments.

I also have a great interest in machine learning and Bayesian statistics. I use some methods from those disciplines in my work and I plan to study for a master's degree in statistics. I enjoy coding a lot, both for work and for fun.
